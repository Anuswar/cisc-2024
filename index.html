ABOUT SMVITM

Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management, Bantakal, situated in the Udupi district, has been at the forefront of providing quality higher education in the coastal Karnataka region. The institute, with roots dating back 700 years to the esteemed Shri Sode Vadiraj Mutt, prides itself on its commitment to excellence by offering a conducive learning environment and consistently updating facilities to align with the latest trends and developments.

SMVITM is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi; approved by AICTE, New Delhi; and recognized by the Government of Karnataka. The institution is a pioneer in technical education, boasting NAAC accreditation and programs accredited by the NBA. SMVITM offers Bachelor's programs in Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Nursing. The institute is a certified member of professional societies like ISTE and IEEE.

Despite its rural location, SMVITM has made remarkable strides in its journey towards excellence, featuring distinguished learning-centric facilities and a focus on staying abreast of the latest industrial trends and academic practices.

ABOUT CISC-2024

CISC-2024 is the inaugural event in an annual series of International Conferences on Computational Intelligence and Smart Communication, organized by Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, Udupi, Karnataka, India. CISC-2024 aims to bring together researchers, academicians, engineers, and industry professionals to share their experiences and exchange ideas on the latest developments in the fields of soft computation, artificial intelligence systems, and smart communication.

The digital revolution has profoundly impacted how people live, work, and communicate. The purpose of CISC-2024 is to discuss the role of computational intelligence in growth and the shaping of society. This international conference offers a unique opportunity to gain insights into the architecture and applications of futuristic technology across multidisciplinary areas and promote scientific growth.

Submission of Paper

Publication: IEEE Conference Proceedings*
(*conference Record #64227 Approved by Bengaluru Section and approval awaited from IEEE Headquarters)

Full paper in IEEE format should be submitted using Microsoft's Conference Management Toolkit (CMT). Manuscripts for CISC 2024 will ONLY be accepted electronically through the CMT online submission system. Please do not send a hard copy of the paper. If you are unable to submit your contribution electronically, please contact us.


All accepted, registered and presented papers will be considered for publication, subject to meeting CISC's scope and quality requirements.


Below are some useful instructions to assist the authors in submitting their papers for CISC 2024.


ORIGINALITY OF SUBMITTED PAPERS:

Papers submitted to CISC 2024 shall contain original work by the author(s) that should not have been published or submitted for publication elsewhere. The anti-plagiarism policy is applicable to all submissions. The author(s) are solely responsible for any plagiarized submission.


PAPER SUBMISSION:

All papers must be prepared and submitted in English in the IEEE Standard two-column conference format of A4 size. Papers must be six to eight pages in length, including texts, Figures, photographs, and references.


The authors are required to use the standard IEEE manuscript template for conferences. The Microsoft Word template can be downloaded from the IEEE conference template page: www.ieee.org


Note:The first page must include the title of the paper, author(s), affiliations, address and text.


Please do not include page numbers on submitted documents.


Failing to conform to the standard format will result in rejection.


PAPER UPLOAD:

You can log in to Microsoft's Conference Management Toolkit using your Username and Password. From the top menu below Author Console, click "Create new submission" and choose any Track to submit the paper. Finally, you fill out all the necessary information concerning the submission, upload the paper and click "submit".


All papers must be submitted in PDF or as a Microsoft Word Document to CMT via: microsoft.com


All submitted papers will be peer-reviewed by the Technical Program Committee (TPC).
